Who exactly is Commander Shepard?

Commander Shepard is the main protagonist of Mass Effect, Mass Effect 2, and Mass Effect 3, whose gender, appearance, skills and pre-service history are all customizable and have impacts on the story. Shepard was born on April 11, 2154, is a graduate of the Systems Alliance N7 special forces program (service no. 5923-AC-2826), a veteran of the Skyllian Blitz, and is initially assigned to the SSV Normandy in 2183 as Executive Officer, with a Systems Alliance rank of Lieutenant Commander. Shepard later becomes the first human to join the Spectres, an elite special task force for the Citadel Council.

What are his most notable achievements?

  • He was the first human to become a Spectre member.
  • During the Skyllian Blitz he rallied the Elysium's collonists against the Batarian invaders, and when the enemy forces broke through the colony's defenses, Shepard singlehandedly held them off and sealed the bridge. Thanks to that he was rewarded with the Star of Terra.
  • He participated in the Battle of the Citadel during which he killed Saren, and destroyed a Reaper vanguard called Sovereign.
  • He stopped Collectors' galaxy-wide campain of human abductions by destroying their main base of operations near the galactic core.
  • During the Reaper invasion he rallied a fleet comprised of many alien species, some of which were their worst enemies like the Quarian and the Geth, and used it to fight the Reapers.
  • He made building the Crucible – an ancient superweapon capable of stopping the Reapers and used it to destroy the reapers, saving all the organic life in Milky Way Galaxy.

Timeline of Commander Shepard's life:

  • 2154 – Commander Shepard is born

  • 2171 – Shepard is officially detected as a biotic and fitted with L3 implants

  • 2172 – Shepard enlists in the Alliance military

  • 2176 – Shepard is decorated with Star of Terra for his heroic deeds at Elysium during the Skyllian Blitz

  • 2183 – Shepard fights Saren and the Geth during the Eden Prime War. In the end he defeats Saren and the Alliance Navy destroys Sovereign, preventing the release of the Reapers. A month after the Battle of the Citadel SSV Normandy is destroyed by unknown assailant, resulting in Commander Shepard's death.

  • 2185 – Commander Shepard is revived by Illusive Man of Cerberus and tasked to investigate the disappearances of entire human colonies in the Terminus Systems. Investigation carried out by Shepard reveals that Collectors are responsible for these abductions. Following that discovery Shepard assembles a team and destroys the Collector homeworld eliminating the Collector threat.

  • 2186 – Reapers invade the galaxy. Commander Shepard escapes invaded Earth and discovers a superweapon called Crucible capable of wiping out the reapers. After that discovery he and his allies rally a fleet comprised of various races of the galaxy. During the final confrontation with the Reapers over the Earth, Shepard uses the crucible to destroy the Reapers and ends the Reaper threat once for all. His fate, as of now, remains unknown.
